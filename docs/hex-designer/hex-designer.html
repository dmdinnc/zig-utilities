How to Use:

  • Select Hexes: Click or drag to activate hexes (they become visible and editable)
  • Colors Tab: Paint activated hex cells and add symbols (circle, crosshair, person)
  • Lines Tab: Draw lines and arrows between hex centers
  • Left-click + drag: Select/paint cells or draw lines between hexes
  • Right-click: Add colored symbols to hex cells (Colors tab) or remove lines (Lines tab)
  • Export: Only activated hexes are exported with a border following the selected shape
